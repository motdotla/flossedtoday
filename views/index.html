Flossed Today

Are you like me and do you forget to floss?

Flossed Today reminds you to floss with a daily email. Enter your email below to get started.



Some History

>> On Dec 15th, 2013 I decided to bring back Flossed Today as an open source app.

This time I rewrote the app from scratch to be a fraction of the amount of lines of code. Also, I open sourced it.

>> On February 4th, 2012 I decided to shut down Flossed Today.

The app was inspired by Thoughtbot's umbrellatoday.com. With reminders from this app I actually started flossing and so did others.

Thank you to all who subscribed previous and subscribe now. Show off those pearly whites!
